Getting started with Minimal Kanban is easy.

Here are some tips to help get you started:

    1. Export your board to a CSV file, and have it sent to your email.
      (This implicitly exports all the lists present on your board!)

    2. Export your lists to a CSV file, and have it sent to your email.
      (This implicitly exports all the cards present on your specific list!)

    3. You can import lists from a CSV file. The CSV file should have the
       following format:

       -> list_name, list_description

    4. You can import cards from a CSV file. The CSV file should have the
       following format:

       -> card_title, card_content, card_deadline, card_status

    11. Summary Page is available.
        It provides a graphical representation of the progress on your
        board.

    12. You can view the total number of cards, completed cards, pending cards
        and overdue cards for each list on the summary page.
